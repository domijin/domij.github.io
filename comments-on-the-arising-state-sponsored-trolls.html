Comments on the arising state-sponsored Trolls

Bloomberg published an article about state-sponsored trolls in a style. It leads to a report by Future Lab. I'm shocked but also pleased for the public awareness it may bring after the heated discussion about net neutrality few month ago.

Equality is the most treasurable property of the internet. It ensures freedom and unalienable rights, that among these are Life, Liberty and the pursuit of Happiness.

We fear the villains will take advantage to spread crimes to unprecedented wide range of the society but on the other hand, hegemonism and despotism find their way to the world.

Anonymous is the protection that ensures equality at first but now the thread to the security of an opening online society that augments the physical world.

We will not be afraid and together, we will find and perfect the techniques to avoid such sociological scam. Among these are differential privacy, blockchain, open source spirit and humanity! We should rethink about anonymity, which is getting outdated in the flatting world and only brings psychological attraction to behave out of box.

Reach me out about building an identification system that should be passed to the next generations and beyond.
